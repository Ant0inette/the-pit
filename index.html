The Pit is a (semi) clandestine literary salon dedicated to writing about sex, love, the erotic and its opposites. Formed in July 2025 by Alexandra Symons-Sutcliffe and Caitlin Hall, The Pit meets regularly in a hotel room in East London. Usually there is no audience or documentation. Readers bring their text anonymously and another participant has to read it aloud. This act of performative dispossession, splitting persona from text, is a little like a parlour game. The hotel meetings are the core of The Pit but we have also organised public sessions including at the ICA and work with artists on experimental ways of documenting the event. Collaborators include: Jenna Bliss, Nina Könnemann, and Zoe Williams.
